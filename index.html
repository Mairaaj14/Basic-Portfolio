About Me

After graduating high school I decided to go to San Francisco State University to major in Psychology and a minor in Latino Studies. Although I liked the path I was taking, I felt like I needed something more exciting for my future life and decided to explore other careers. I decided to switch it up and take some classes about Computer Science and also Business Administration. In the upcoming semester, I took an intro to Computer Science course which I loved and decided to completely change my career path and start getting more into coding. Diving into UC Berkeley's Coding Bootcamp to become a full stack developer and be a master in both back and front end.
